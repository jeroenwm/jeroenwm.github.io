About me

I’m Jeroen, one of your future colleagues. Even though I initially started out studies in IT, I quickly noticed coding wasn’t exactly my cup of tea and ended up getting a masters degree in communcation sciences. After a first job in the marketing department of the Antwerp Zoo, I started working in the music business where I’ve been spending the rest of my career up to now. Before that I was already active as a dj and event promotor, but as a professional I’ve spent my time at record labels. I’ve been involved in different roles spanning marketing, communications, strategy and A&R. 

Like many of you I felt it was time to switch something up in my career. In my case, after 10 years, I felt the need to balance out my life with something different besides music related projects. I was looking for something analythical, challenging and interesting where I’d be able to grow and learn some new skills along the way. The idea of becoming a functional analyst had come up before, so I was excited to find out about this program and the opportunity it presented. 

On a more personal note

I grew up in Beringen (Limburg), studied in Leuven, lived in Gent for about 8 years, currently live in Antwerp and will be moving in to our house in Wemmel soon. 

Apart from my obvious passion for (electronic) music, I’m a science fiction buff, love to cook, prefer roadtripping through the USA, have been a gamer for ages, love everything space related and I’m always in for a good movie or tv show. 

Next to some musical projects, I’m hosting a radio show, raising my second puppy and preparing the move.
